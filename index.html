Automated logging

Jumo harnesses RFID technology to log student athlete practice hours. This is handled in the back end so coaches now only need to tap in and out with an RFID card and we do the rest.

Designed for coaches

Logging practice hours for a whole team is not only a hassle for coaches, but it also cuts into their busy schedule. With Jumo, coaches no longer need to spend hours of their time trying to remember what time Johnny practiced.

Maximum accuracy

RFID technology removes the human error that the world is prone to. The old days of mistakenly logging incorrect log sheets are no more.

About

We are a team of undergraduate college students in the Informatics department at the University of Washington. We have partnered up with the Intercollegiate Athletic department here at UW to replace a current inefficient athletically related logging system.
